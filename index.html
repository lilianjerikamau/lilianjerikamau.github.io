Electric Vehicle Mobile Application

This innovative app utilizes advanced OCR technology to effortlessly capture vehicle odometer readings. It enables accurate calculations of SODO (State of Drive Operations), SODA (State of Drive Analysis), and SOECS (State of Electric Charge Status). Users can easily capture odometer readings using their smartphone camera, eliminating manual input errors. The app provides valuable insights into vehicle performance, driving patterns, and electric charge status. Additionally, it features an interactive map for locating nearby charging stations. This app revolutionizes tracking vehicle metrics and optimizing the electric vehicle experience.
